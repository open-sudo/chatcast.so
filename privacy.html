2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Your contact details, including email address and WhatsApp phone number
  • Chat content and uploaded media (e.g., images, music, documents)
  • Metadata such as timestamps, IP addresses, and device/browser type

We may also collect information via the YouTube API Services to facilitate publishing on your behalf.


3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate, maintain, and improve our services. This includes:

  • Generating, editing, and publishing podcast-style videos to platforms like YouTube
  • Analyzing user behavior to improve our AI and user experience
  • Personalizing features and recommendations

We may also use data in aggregated, anonymized formats for analytics and product enhancement.


4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

We also use cookies or similar technologies that may collect data from your device or browser.


5. Data Retention and Security

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes outlined. Your data is protected using reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures. While we strive for high standards, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data processed via YouTube API Services is governed by Google's data retention and sharing policies.

6. Your Rights and Choices
